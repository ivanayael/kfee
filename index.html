About Us

Kfee is a cafeteria venture that emerged to support homeless people. We bring you hot drinks during the fall and winter and cold drinks during the spring and summer. We also transform the proceeds into food. Every coffee you donate becomes direct help. Help us build a better world with a simple gesture: donating your virtual coffee.
